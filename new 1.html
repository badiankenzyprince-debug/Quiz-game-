Lesson 1: Vocabulary

Word: Improve Meaning: To make something better
Word: Confident Meaning: Feeling sure about yourself
Word: Practice Meaning: Repeating something to get better

Lesson 2: Grammar Tip

Present Simple Tense is used for daily activities and facts.

Example: “I study English every day.”

  • I read books.
  • She watches movies.
  • They practice speaking.

Lesson 3: Reading Practice

Learning English takes time and practice. Reading, listening, and speaking every day will help you improve faster. Do not be afraid to make mistakes.
